About

osean was launched in 2006. It's never-seen before attractions set amidst unique architecture with international experience, standard amenities and hygiene; catapult osean into a different league. It is the ultimate entertainment package for people of all age groups and is built innovatively to provide tons of excitement. It has some of the mind-blowing water rides, slides and other fun activities, the park also has a number of restaurants that promise lip-smacking delights. It is a perfect family play port with different play elements. A visit to osean must be on your bucket list.
